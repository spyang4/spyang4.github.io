About Me

Hello and welcome to my website! Four years ago, I came to the University of Wisconsin – Madison with no clue what kind of engineering major I would follow. I ended up in Computer Engineering but still had no idea that I would become the president of the UW-Madison section of the Society of Women Engineers, be able to build a program that can navigate a robot autonomously through a maze, and fall in love with web development. I’m now a fifth year senior—graduating this December—in Computer Engineering and Computer Science, and I am proud to say that I have learned how to be an engineer and a leader throughout my time as a student.
